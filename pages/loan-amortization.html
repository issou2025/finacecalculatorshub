Understanding Loan Amortization

Loan amortization is the process of paying off a debt over time through regular payments. Each payment consists of both principal and interest, with the proportion of principal increasing over time while the interest portion decreases.

Key Components

  • Principal Payment: Reduces the loan balance
  • Interest Payment: Cost of borrowing
  • Total Payment: Principal + Interest
  • Remaining Balance: Outstanding loan amount

Using the Schedule

  • Track payment breakdown over time
  • Plan for extra principal payments
  • Understand interest costs
  • Monitor loan payoff progress
