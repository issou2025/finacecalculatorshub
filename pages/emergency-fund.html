Understanding Emergency Funds

An emergency fund is a financial safety net that helps you deal with unexpected expenses or loss of income. Having adequate emergency savings can help you avoid debt and maintain financial stability during challenging times.

What to Include in Monthly Expenses

  • Housing (rent/mortgage, utilities)
  • Food and groceries
  • Transportation costs
  • Insurance premiums
  • Minimum debt payments
  • Essential personal care

Emergency Fund Tips

  • Keep funds easily accessible in a savings account
  • Start small and build gradually
  • Replenish the fund after using it
  • Review and adjust as expenses change

When to Use Emergency Funds

  • Job loss or reduced income
  • Medical emergencies
  • Urgent home or car repairs
  • Unexpected travel needs
