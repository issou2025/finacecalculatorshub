Understanding Mortgage Payments

Your monthly mortgage payment typically includes:

  • Principal - The amount you're borrowing
  • Interest - The cost of borrowing money
  • Property Taxes (optional)
  • Home Insurance (optional)

Tips for Lower Payments

  • Make a larger down payment
  • Shop for better interest rates
  • Consider a longer term
  • Improve your credit score
