Planning for Retirement

Retirement planning is crucial for ensuring financial security in your later years. This calculator helps you determine how much you need to save monthly to reach your retirement goals, taking into account your current savings and expected investment returns.

Retirement Planning Tips

  • Start saving early to benefit from compound interest
  • Consider inflation when setting your target amount
  • Diversify your retirement investments
  • Review and adjust your plan regularly

Common Retirement Accounts

  • 401(k) - Employer-sponsored retirement plan
  • Traditional IRA - Tax-deferred retirement account
  • Roth IRA - Tax-free growth retirement account
  • SEP IRA - Self-employed retirement option
